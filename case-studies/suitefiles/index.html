SuiteFiles

SuiteFiles’ total document management software helps busy professional services teams ditch the admin and simplify their workflows, so they can focus on doing work that delights their clients. Serving over 12,000 users around the world, SuiteFiles saves teams an average of five hours per staff member, per week.

I lead marketing at this B2B SaaS startup, with responsibility for strategy and execution across all areas of this function.

Introduction

When I joined SuiteFiles in 2024, my priorities were working collaboratively to build trust, credibility, and relationships between our go-to-market and product teams; establishing the product marketing sub-function; and creating and implementing a cohesive strategy to bring together marketing activities across demand generation, content marketing, product marketing, and SDR roles.

Highlights have included driving 30% year-on-year growth in qualified sales opportunities, delivering the strongest quarter of direct demo bookings since 2023, and leading a comprehensive rebrand and website rebuild aimed at re-establishing SuiteFiles’ credibility and positioning in-market.
